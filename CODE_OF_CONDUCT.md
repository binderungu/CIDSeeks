# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We expect contributors and reviewers to:

- be respectful and professional
- focus on technical substance and reproducible evidence
- provide constructive feedback
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- abusive or insulting language
- harassment or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions,
and other project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
privately and include relevant context (links, screenshots, timestamps).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, or restrict participation for
violations, with severity proportional to the behavior.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant approach and simplified for
this research software project.
